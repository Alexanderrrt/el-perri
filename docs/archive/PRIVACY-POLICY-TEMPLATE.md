## 1. Introduction

El Perri Latin Food ("Company," "we," "us," "our") operates an online restaurant ordering platform and related services. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information in compliance with applicable privacy laws, including:

- **General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)** (EU)

- **Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)**

This Privacy Policy explains:
- What personal information we collect
- How we use and share your information

- How to contact us about privacy concerns

**By using our services, you agree to this Privacy Policy.** If you do not agree, please do not use our platform.

## 2. What Personal Information Do We Collect?

### 2.1 Information You Provide Directly

When you place an order, create an account, or contact us, we collect:

**Account Registration:**
- Name, email address, phone number
- Password (encrypted)
- Delivery address
- Billing address
- Payment method (we do not store card numbers; our payment processor does)
- Profile preferences (dietary restrictions, favorite items)

**Order Information:**
- Items ordered, quantities, and prices
- Delivery address and delivery time preferences
- Special instructions or dietary requirements
- Order frequency and payment method

**Communications:**
- Emails, chat messages, phone calls with customer support
- Survey responses and feedback
- Marketing preferences and opt-in/opt-out status

**Payment Information:**
- We use PCI-compliant payment processors (Stripe, Square) and do NOT store full credit card numbers on our servers
- Payment processors retain card data per their privacy policies

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically

**Device & Browsing Data:**
- IP address
- Browser type and version
- Operating system
- Pages visited and time spent
- Links clicked
- Referral source
- Cookies and tracking pixels

**Location Data:**
- GPS location (only if you enable location services for delivery)
- Approximate location from IP address

**Guest Checkout Data:**
- Email address
- Phone number
- Delivery address
- For marketing purposes only: email address is collected to enable order tracking and marketing communications

### 2.3 Information from Third Parties

- **Payment Processors:** Stripe, Square (payment confirmation, card verification)
- **Delivery Services:** Address verification services
- **Marketing Partners:** Email marketing providers (Mailchimp, etc.)
- **Public Records:** For fraud prevention or legal compliance

### 2.4 Data You Choose NOT to Provide

You may use our website without creating an account. However, you must provide:
- Email address for order confirmation
- Delivery address for delivery orders
- Phone number for order updates

If you do not provide this information, you cannot place an order.

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to:
- Fulfill your orders and provide customer service

- Resolve disputes

**Specific Retention Periods:**

## 7. Your Privacy Rights

### 7.1 GDPR Rights (Applies Worldwide, Including California)

**If you are in the EU or similar jurisdiction:**

1. **Right to Access:** Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you
2. **Right to Rectification:** Correct inaccurate information
3. **Right to Erasure ("Right to Be Forgotten"):** Request deletion of your data
4. **Right to Restrict Processing:** Limit how we use your data
5. **Right to Data Portability:** Download your data in a portable format (JSON/PDF)
6. **Right to Object:** Opt-out of marketing or certain processing
7. **Right to Lodge a Complaint:** File a complaint with your data protection authority

**To exercise these rights:** Contact privacy@elperrilatinfood.com with details of your request.

We will respond within **30 days** of receiving your request.

---

### 7.2 CCPA/CPRA Rights (California Residents)

**If you are a California resident, you have the right to:**

1. **Right to Know:** Request what personal information we collect and how we use it
   - Submit request: privacy@elperrilatinfood.com or phone (408) 582-2502
   - We will provide a detailed disclosure within **45 days**

2. **Right to Delete:** Request deletion of your personal information
   - We will delete your account and personal data within **45 days**
   - Exception: We may keep data needed for tax/accounting (7 years)
   - Your order history remains for financial reporting but is anonymized

3. **Right to Opt-Out of Sale/Sharing of Personal Information**
   - We do NOT sell your data to third parties
   - We do NOT share data with third-party marketers
   - If this changes, we will provide a "Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information" link on our website

4. **Right to Limit Use:** Request we limit how we use your sensitive personal information
   - Submit request: privacy@elperrilatinfood.com
   - We will honor reasonable requests

5. **Right to Correction:** Request we correct inaccurate information
   - Edit your profile directly in your account, or
   - Contact: privacy@elperrilatinfood.com

6. **Right to Non-Discrimination:** You have the right to:
   - Equal service and pricing regardless of privacy choices
   - Not be penalized for exercising privacy rights
   - Receive loyalty rewards even if you opt-out of marketing

**CCPA Verification:**
- We will verify your identity (email confirmation, name matching) before fulfilling data requests
- For security, we may require additional verification (last 4 digits of phone number, etc.)

## 8. Security & Data Protection

### 8.1 How We Protect Your Data

- **Encryption:** All data transmitted over HTTPS (TLS 1.2+)
- **Password Security:** Passwords are hashed with bcrypt/Argon2
- **Database Security:** Encrypted at rest (AES-256)
- **Access Controls:** Employee access limited to necessary personnel only
- **Regular Audits:** Annual security reviews and penetration testing

### 8.2 What We Cannot Guarantee

Despite our security measures, no system is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee:
- Absolute protection against hacking or data breaches
- Protection against user-compromised credentials (weak passwords)

**If a data breach occurs:**
- We will notify all affected users within **30 days** (or as required by law)
- We will provide free credit monitoring for 1 year
- We will cooperate with law enforcement investigations

### 8.3 You Are Responsible For:
- Keeping your password confidential
- Logging out on shared devices
- Immediately reporting unauthorized access
- Verifying billing statements for unauthorized charges

## 18. Definitions

- **Processing:** Any action taken on personal information (collection, storage, use, sharing, deletion)

- **Consent:** Affirmative, informed agreement to processing
- **Data Subject:** Any person whose information we collect
- **Data Controller:** El Perri (determines how data is processed)
- **Data Processor:** Third-party service providers (payment processors, email platforms)
- **GDPR:** General Data Protection Regulation (EU law)
